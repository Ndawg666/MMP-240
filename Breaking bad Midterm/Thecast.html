The Cast of Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad features a talented cast of actors who brought the characters to life:

  • Bryan Cranston as Walter White
  • Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman
  • Anna Gunn as Skylar White
  • Dean Norris as Hank Schrader

Other Notable Characters

Additionally, the series includes several other memorable characters portrayed by various actors:

  • Giancarlo Esposito as Gustavo "Gus" Fring
  • Jonathan Banks as Mike Ehrmantraut
  • Bob Odenkirk as Saul Goodman
  • Betsy Brandt as Marie Schrader
