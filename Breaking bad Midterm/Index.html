About Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American television series created by Vince Gilligan in 2008. It follows the story of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, and his descent into the criminal underworld.

Main Characters

  • Walter White
  • Jesse Pinkman
  • Skylar White
  • Hank Schrader

Episodes

The series consists of five seasons with a total of 62 episodes. It received critical acclaim and won numerous awards, including 16 Primetime Emmy Awards.

Legacy

Breaking Bad is often regarded as one of the best shows of all time. It has a significant impact on popular culture and has paved the way for a prequel series named "Better Call Saul".
