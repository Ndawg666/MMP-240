Easy Brownie Recipie

  • Prep Time: 10 Minutes
  • Cook Time:20-30 Minutes
  • Total Time: 30-40 Minutes
  • Servings: 16
Brownie Refrence

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups White Sugar
  • 1 1/2 Cups all purpose flour
  • 1 Cup melted butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup cocoa powder
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  3. Spread the batter evenly into the greased pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-30 minutes, until the top is dry and the edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan.
  5. Cool before slicing into squares and serving.

Nutrition facts
